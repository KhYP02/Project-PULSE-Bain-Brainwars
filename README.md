# Project PULSE — AI Wearable Expansion Strategy for India

Project PULSE was developed as part of Bain Brainwars 2026, focusing on how an AI-powered wearable ecosystem could scale successfully in India through strategic product sequencing, GTM planning, and AI-driven retention loops.

## What This Project Covers

* Market Expansion Strategy
* GTM Planning
* Product Sequencing
* Unit Economics
* Financial Modeling
* AI Subscription Flywheel
* Competitive Moat Analysis
* Risk Mitigation
* Capital Allocation

## Key Outcomes

### Part 1

* Identified India as highest ROI expansion market
* Projected:

  * $94.1M revenue
  * $14.7M EBITDA
  * 490% ROI

### Part 2

* Built Ring → Band → Watch rollout strategy
* Designed AI-driven ecosystem moat
* Projected:

  * $126.8M revenue
  * $31.9M EBITDA
  * 266% ROI
